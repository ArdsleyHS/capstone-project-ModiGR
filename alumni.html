Famous Alumni from Columbia University

In 1981, Obama transferred to Columbia University from Occidental College in search of a closer connection to the African American community, according to his memoir Dreams from My Father: A Story of Race and Inheritance. He graduated in 1983 with a major in political science.

Franklin Delano Roosevelt entered Columbia Law School in 1905 but left in 1907, after passing the bar exam, to practice law.

Mr. Milonovich is currently working on a doctoral degree from Teachers College, Columbia University in mathematics education.
