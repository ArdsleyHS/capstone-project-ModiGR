Columbia University

Columbia University is a a private Ivy League university based in New York City. Founded in 1754 as a royal charter of King George II of Great Britain, it’s the oldest Education Institution in the state, and the 5th oldest in the nation. Major education publications consistently rate Columbia among one of the top universities in the world!
